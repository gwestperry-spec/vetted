How it works

Job offers don't deserve hunches.

  1. Build your filter framework.

    Five filters or more — you decide what matters. Comp, scope, access, timing, culture, location, growth. Set the weights once. Adjust whenever your career posture shifts.

  2. Paste a job — or a URL.

    Drop the JD or link the listing. Vetted parses the role and runs it against your framework, not a generic rubric.

  3. One verdict you can defend.

    Pursue the opportunity, monitor the opportunity, or pass on it entirely — with a VQ Score, an honest fit summary, the real gaps, and the comp picture to back it up.
